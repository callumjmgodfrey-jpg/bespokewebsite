Lifetime
Repairs

If a pair of jeans I've made ever needs fixing, I'll repair it. No questions, no expiry date. You cover shipping both ways — that's it.

What's covered

Seam failures, broken hardware, blown-out crotch seams, waistband repairs, re-hemming — anything that's a manufacturing or wear issue.

What's not covered

Damage from misuse, alterations done by someone else, or repairs requested after significant modification to the original garment. Use your judgement — if you're not sure, just ask.

Cost

Labour is free. You pay shipping to Wellington and back. That's the whole deal.

Turnaround

I'll assess and repair as quickly as I can. Expect 1–2 weeks from when I receive them, depending on what needs doing.

How to send them

Email me first with a photo and a description of the issue. I'll confirm, give you the address, and we'll sort the rest from there.

Got a pair that needs attention? Drop me a line — include a photo and a brief description of what's happened.
